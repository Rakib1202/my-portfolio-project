I’m MD.Rakib Hossain, a final-year Software Engineering student majoring in Robotics at Daffodil International University, Bangladesh. While my academic focus is robotics, I have developed strong skills in full-stack web development, building scalable and user-centric applications using technologies like JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express.js, Python, Java, and MySQL. I’ve completed numerous frontend and backend projects, which I’ve carefully documented and stored on my GitHub for easy access and review. These projects reflect my hands-on experience and dedication to writing clean, efficient code. Alongside coding, I bring over three years of video editing experience with Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Photoshop, supporting my creative side in design and multimedia. I’m fluent in Bangla, English, and Hindi, and currently learning German to prepare for international opportunities. My long-term goal is to leverage my full-stack development expertise alongside my robotics background to contribute to innovative tech solutions and pursue higher studies in Europe focused on robotics and intelligent automation.
